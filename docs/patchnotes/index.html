0.5.4b Patch Notes

Boss Improvements

  • Removed a majority of the stun and freeze immunity windows from all versions of the Tor Gul, the Defiler boss fight.
  • Improved the Minion Phase in the Tasgul, Swallower of Light boss fight.
  • Fixed a bug where It That Was Esh could sometimes fail to advance phases properly.
  • Fixed a bug where the Arbiter of Divinity could rarely drop loot in an unreachable location.
  • Defeating the Arbiter of Divinity now re-enables the exit elevator.
  • Extended the time the player has to get into the first Flame Seed during the combo skill that begins with the Large Laser in the Arbiter of Ash boss fight.
  • The voiceline used by the Arbiter of Ash when doing his skill which creates lanes of fiery death now plays at the start of the skill, instead of after he rises up to the air.
  • Made the following improvements to the Vessel of Kulemak boss fight:
    • Fixed an issue where it could get stuck after its Teleport Slam skill during the final phase of the fight.
    • Disabled the skill used during the final phase of the fight that summoned minions.
    • The Volatiles spawned during the final phase of the fight now spawn in bursts, with a significantly longer time between each burst. This is to allow for more time to fight and damage the boss.
    • Improved the visual feedback of the portals during the final phase. Further improvements to these will be coming at a later time.
    • Adjusted the blocking of the abyss in the middle of the arena during the final phase to more closely reflect the actual shape.
    • Fixed a bug where after killing the Vessel of Kulemak for the first time in a given instance, reviving at checkpoint would not put players in a new instance.
  • Made the following improvements to the Arbiter of Divinity boss fight:
    • The voicelines used by the boss are now guaranteed for his notable skills, as opposed to having a low chance to play.
    • The voicelines used by the boss now have less variance, so you can better correlate specific lines to specific skills.
    • The Divinity Clones created during the second phase of the fight now have a significantly larger hitbox allowing them to more easily be hit and killed while you have the Divinity buff.
    • Increased the windows of time between certain attacks and many of the combo skills to make dodge rolling these skills more consistent. This is also accompanied by a number of visual telegraphing improvements and reduction in aggression on target tracking speed.
  • Made the following improvements to the Phya, Sentinel of the Cradle boss fight in The Matriarch Halls:
    • Improved telegraphing and visuals of the Dashing Sunders skill.
    • The Dashing Sunders skill now correctly deals Physical damage, instead of Cold Damage.
    • Reduced the frequency and count of projectiles fired by the Generator.
    • Removed the chilling ground from the projectiles fired by the Generator.
    • The Generator will now stop firing projectiles during the Gravity Well and the Turret Sunder phases.
    • The Gravity Well phase will now correctly hit you if you fail to make it to the safe zones anywhere in the arena. Previously you could avoid this by hiding behind the spheres in the four corners.
    • Gravity Wells can no longer be created outside of the arena.
    • If you fail the Gravity Well phase while Shapeshifted you are now transformed back into a human and thrown into the air. Previously nothing would happen to your character and you'd just take damage without knowing why.
    • Improved the visuals of the 'dash' skills to look more like a teleport as intended.

Expedition Improvements

  • Logbooks that add a Boss Expedition now prioritise replacing regular Maps over Grand Expeditions.
  • Modifiers in Grand Expedition now appear in the map stats list when inside the area.
  • Added an additional option to the Expedition Atlas Tree "Double or Nothing" Notable, allowing players to select no change if desired.
  • Fixed a bug where some monsters in Expeditions could become invisible and invincible.
  • Fixed an issue where Medved, Vorana, Uhtred and Olroth were not considered Powerful Map Bosses. This means they also now have notably more Life.
  • Fixed a bug where Verisium Sentry minimap icons would not disappear upon activating them.
  • Fixed a bug where the Barren Atoll Map did not have a Grand Expedition Atlas Marker.
  • Fixed a bug where Underground Areas in Expeditions and Grand Expeditions could sometimes spawn with all their monsters dead. This would occur if the underground area was opened with an explosive that also activated a Remnant encounter and that Remnant encounter had the Life Rune. The monsters in the underground area would then have their life shared with the Remnant monsters and would thus be killed when the Remnant monsters were defeated. This was also why Styrn, Fallen Knight of Aldur would sometimes be dead when entering his cave.
  • Fixed a bug that prevented players from rerolling Remnants when using a controller.
  • Fixed a bug where the loot from Underground Areas in Expeditions and Grand Expeditions could be redirected to a Verisium Remnant. This would cause the monsters in the underground areas to appear to drop no items.
  • Fixed an issue where underground areas in Grand Expedition could sometimes be accessed without detonating the entrance.
  • Fixed a bug where Oath Rune could spawn monsters in blocked or inaccessible areas.
  • Fixed a bug with Verisium Remnants in Grand Expeditions where monsters (without Oath Rune necessarily) could spawn out-of-bounds and prevent completion.
  • Fixed a bug where the following Grand Expedition areas could be attempted infinitely: Craggy Peninsula, Lush Isle, Frigid Bluffs, Barren Atoll, and Exhumed Ruins.

  • Monsters Corrupted by the Offerings to the Queen Atlas Passive now have 100% increased Rarity of Items Dropped (from 50%).
  • The Distant Origins Atlas Passive Skill now notifies the player when it reveals The Ezomyte Megaliths on their Atlas.

The Curse of the Allflame Challenge League

  • Meet Valerie, a dangerous corsair captain who needs your help retrieving long forgotten treasures from the icy depths of Wraeclast's oceans. Find Charts scattered across Wraeclast, board The Sovereign, and set sail!
  • Dive to the ocean floor where lanterns imbued with the spark of the Allflame will shield you from the crushing waves. Explore and claim what you can while you can! Lost gold, valuable chests, and lurking threats await you...
  • Upon charting enough areas, connect them together to create a Voyage — one giant seafloor excursion. Revisit each chart to claim its rewards again, while also amplifying those rewards in several different ways.
  • Head belowdecks, and you'll find the heart of the ship at work. Feed it an item, and it will split into a multitude of ghostly outcomes, one of which you can keep for yourself.
  • Find valuable Ducats on the seafloor and use them to warp and twist items in ways not seen in centuries.
  • For more information about this expansion, check out pathofexile.com/allflame
  • With 3.29.0, there are Standard, Hardcore and Solo Self-Found variations of the Curse of the Allflame challenge league available. They have the same core mechanics and items. You can create private league versions of these leagues, with mods that make the game harder.
  • The Hardcore variations of Curse of the Allflame are parented to their Standard Curse of the Allflame equivalent, meaning if you die in Hardcore Curse of the Allflame you can continue on in the Standard Curse of the Alfflame League.
  • The new Curse of the Allflame challenge league includes a set of 40 new challenges, and 8 new challenges in Ruthless Curse of the Allflame. There are microtransaction rewards for completing challenges that are only obtainable in this league. These rewards will be revealed in an upcoming news post.
  • From the 19th challenge onwards and for every third challenge after that, you will receive pieces of the Curse of the Allflame Totem Pole decoration to display in your hideout. The Totem Pole permanently showcases how many of the Curse of the Allflame challenges you completed during the league.

New Content and Features

  • Introduced two new Bloodline Classes, one which is exclusive to the Curse of the Allflame challenge league.
  • Added a new Exceptional Skill Gem, Pact of Beidat: Cast a pact, Empowering spells that either fire projectiles, apply an effect to an area around a targeted location or fire chaining beams, causing them deal more damage and have enhanced coverage. However, using this skill also burdens you with an Affliction.
  • Added a new Exceptional Skill Gem, Pact of Ghorr: Cast a pact, Empowering your damage over time spells but burdening you with an Affliction. This skill also grants the Hunger of Ghorr buff for a duration causing Fleshrend to trigger periodically.
  • Added a new Exceptional Skill Gem, Pact of K'tash: Cast a pact, Empowering your damaging Vaal spells but burdening you with an Affliction. Empowered Vaal spells deal more damage, have less soul gain prevention duration and refund souls on use.
  • Added a new Exceptional Skill Gem, Pact of Lycia: Cast a pact, Empowering the next channelling spells you cast yourself but burdening you with an Affliction. Empowered spells deal more damage and periodically trigger lightning bolts while channelling.
  • Added a new Exceptional Support Gem, Coursing Currents: Supports skills that hit enemies, providing increased effect of Chill and Shock inflicted with Supported Skills, causing enemies to be chilled when they are shocked by your hits, and shocked when they are chilled by your hits.
  • Added a new Exceptional Support Gem, Crystalfall: Supported Skills will Trigger Falling Crystal on Hit, causing a giant crystal of abyssal soulstone to be called from above, falling to slam down at the targeted location. Supports Slam skills. Cannot support triggered skills or skills used by things other than you.
  • Added a new Intelligence/Strength Skill, Mana-Infused Staff: Infuses your staff with arcane energy, lowering your mana costs and increasing your chance to block. When you block, you release an arcane wave, damaging enemies in a ring-shaped area over a duration.
  • Added a new Strength/Intelligence Transfigured Skill, Divine Blast of Radiance: Project a beam of holy light from your shield to a targeted location, causing an implosion of holy energy. This energy then lingers for a short duration as an orb, periodically releasing larger explosions. Increases and reductions to attack speed affect the rate at which explosions occur.
  • Added a new Strength/Intelligence Transfigured Skill, Holy Hammers of Spirals: Slam the ground, consuming all Power Charges and calling down a cascade of holy hammers from above in an outward spiral. Further hammers are called down for each Power Charge consumed. Requires a Mace, Sceptre or Staff.
  • Added a new Strength/Intelligence Transfigured Skill, Holy Sweep of Hammerfalls: Swings a two handed mace or a staff in a circle, calling down waves of holy hammers for a duration. Increases and reductions to Attack Speed will affect the rate at which these hammerfalls occur.
  • Added a new Strength/Intelligence Transfigured Skill, Reap of Butchery: A bloody scythe swipes around you, applying a physical damage over time debuff and hitting enemies with physical damage. If any survive, you gain a blood charge which raises the damage and cost of the skill. Players can have 5 maximum blood charges.
  • Added 15 new Unique Items.
  • Added 3 new Divination Cards.
  • Added the keywording and popup pinning system used in Path of Exile 2, allowing you to inspect certain words for more information. We will gradually be making more use of these systems in future patches.
  • Added the new Pearlescent Amulet base item type which has an all Elemental Resistances Implicit Modifier.
  • When playing in a party, any player is now able to interact with the Expedition Detonator, Eldritch Altars, Harvest Plots, and Sentinels.
  • Volatile Vaal Orbs and Crystallised Rancour now have dedicated slots in the Currency Stash Tab.
  • Added 2 new crafting bench options, unlocked by default. Reroll 3 Modifiers on a Rare Item for a cost of 3 Chaos Orbs, and Reroll a Modifier on a Rare Item for a cost of 8 Chaos Orbs.
  • Isla in Kingsmarch, Niko in the Mine Encampment and Einhar in the Menagerie are now all able to identify items for you.
  • There is now a Crafting Bench available in every Town from Act 2 onwards.
  • Catalysts now have a maximum stack size of 20 (previously 10).
  • The audio for most Raised Spectres should now be quieter.
  • The waypoint in The Solaris Temple Level 1 in Act 8 has been moved closer to the Level 2 entrance.
  • Continued to incrementally improve the sound, art, effects and environments.

Mercenaries of Trarthus as a Core League

  • Mercenaries from the lawless cities of Trarthus can now be found in areas from Act 3 onwards, awaiting you to accept their duel.
  • Wager for an item the Mercenary is carrying, if you lose, the gold you levied will be forfeited and the Mercenary teleports out. Win, however, and the Mercenary will aid you as an ally for a number of areas, in addition to winning the spoils of course.
  • You are guaranteed to find a Mercenary in the following Campaign Areas: The Sceptre of God, The Grand Arena, The Ridge, and The Hidden Underbelly. Mercenaries found in these areas will not have any Rucksack items.
  • Hired Mercenaries will scale up their passive stats and skills to the level of the current area you are in, up to a maximum of level 68. The level of items you can equip to your Mercenary is still capped by the level of the area where you found that Mercenary.
  • In Map areas, if you don't like anything the Mercenary offers, you're able to request their Warrant. A Warrant is a promise to return and duel again at a later date – a call to action which summons that specific Mercenary to a future Map, with the same set of Skills, but a new set of items. Warrants also allow you to trade specific Mercenaries to other players. A Mercenary will only grant their Warrant once however, preventing you from fighting that Mercenary in every single area.
  • Unlike in the Mercenaries of Trarthus League, having a Mercenary does not count as a player party member, so having a Mercenary does not increase the Monster Life, or the Quantity or Rarity of Items dropped from Monsters.
  • Added the new Luminary Ascendancy Class for the Scion, which allows you to permanently Hire Mercenaries. Luminaries can have up to 3 permanently hired Mercenaries, 2 in reserve in Towns and Hideouts, and 1 as an active ally in combat. Unlike with regular Mercenaries, you can change the items permanently hired Mercenaries have equipped, opening up innumerate potential build possibilities.
  • The following Unique items are now once again obtained through Mercenary encounters, and have been removed from the core drop pool: Azadi Crest, Binds of Bloody Vengeance, The Ghastly Theatre, Hand of Heresy, Howlcrack, and Scornflux.
  • Trarthan Gems are no longer possible rewards from Gem Chests in Heist. They again are obtained from Mercenary encounters.
  • Added support for Mercenaries of Trarthus to the Atlas Passive Tree.
  • Added a series of Trarthan Scarabs:
    • Trarthan Scarab: Area is inhabited by a Mercenary.
    • Trarthan Scarab of Infamy: Mercenaries found in Area are Infamous, Mercenaries found in Area are accompanied by two Wild Mercenaries.
    • Trarthan Scarab of Renown: All Equipment Items on Mercenaries found in Area are Unique.
    • Trarthan Scarab of Surprising Alliances: Wild Rogue Exiles in Area have 50% chance to be accompanied by a Wild Mercenary, Wild Mercenaries have increased Difficulty for each Wild Mercenary in Area.
  • Mercenary Rucksacks in Endgame Maps can no longer generate Orbs of Transmutation or Orbs of Augmentation.
  • Added Searing Exarch Altar Modifiers that drop Mercenaries Scarabs.
  • Hired Mercenaries now have an information sheet with a list of all their passive stats.

Abyss Revamp

  • Abyss has received function changes to the mechanic to improve the gameplay significantly.
  • Abyssal Pits, and the cracks leading up to them, are now already opened before you approach, subjugating the souls of the enemies unlucky enough to be within its influence.
  • Slaying these enemies sends their souls into the pit, drawing the attention and ire of the minions of Kulemak dwelling beneath. Upon slaying enough of them, the Abyssal legion will spill forth from their domain.
  • Gone are the days of chasing a meandering Abyssal crack around a Map, often backwards.
  • Alongside this revamp, a new Abyss Endgame Pinnacle Boss has been added, with its own set of brand new Unique items.
  • Abyss is now the exclusive source of Abyssal Jewels and Stygian Vises, though Abyssal Jewels can still be obtained rarely within Abyss Biomes in Delve.
  • Abyssal Delirium Orbs can no longer be obtained.
  • Abyssal Jewels can no longer roll increased Damage with Hits and Ailments against Abyssal Monsters, Minions deal increased Damage with Hits and Ailments against Abyssal Monsters, additional Physical Damage Reduction against Abyssal Monsters, or additional Physical Damage Reduction if you weren't Damaged by a Hit Recently.
  • The base Abyss Scarab now has a limit of 5 from 2. If an area already has an Abyss, modifiers that cause areas to contain additional Abysses will instead add two additional pits.
  • The Abyss Scarab of Multitudes now grants Abyss Chasms in Area spawn 100% increased Monsters per Soul fed.
  • The Abyss Scarab of Edifice has been renamed to Abyss Scarab of Crystals and causes Abyss Pits in Area that do not contain a reward instead create an Abyssal Crystal.
  • The Abyss Scarab of Profound Depth has been renamed to Abyssal Scarab of the Consort and causes one Abyss Pit in Area to spawn an Abyssal Consort.
  • The following changes have been made to the highest tier Modifiers for Abyssal Jewels, their lower tier counterparts have been adjusted accordingly:
    • 20% increased Effect of Cold Ailments (previously up to 15%).
    • 20% increased Effect of Lightning Ailments (previously up to 15%).
    • 8% increased Impale Effect (previously up to 6%). Impale Effect no longer rolls on Hypnotic or Ghastly Eye Jewels.
    • +34 to maximum Mana (previously up to +40).
    • 5% of Damage taken Recouped as Mana (previously up to 3%).
    • Adds 30 to 42 Fire Damage to Spells (previously up to 23 to 32).
    • Adds 28 to 38 Cold Damage to Spells (previously up to 23 to 32).
    • Adds 8 to 59 Lightning Damage to Spells (previously up to 6 to 45).
    • Adds 23 to 32 Physical Damage to Spells (previously up to 16 to 22).
    • Adds 23 to 32 Chaos Damage to Spells (previously up to 16 to 22).
    • Adds 33 to 44 Fire Damage to Spells while holding a Shield/wielding a Two Handed Weapon/Dual Wielding (previously up to 25 to 34).
    • Adds 20 to 41 Cold Damage to Spells while holding a Shield/wielding a Two Handed Weapon/Dual Wielding (previously up to 25 to 34).
    • Adds 8 to 66 Lightning Damage to Spells while holding a Shield/wielding a Two Handed Weapon/Dual Wielding (previously up to 6 to 51).
    • Adds 24 to 35 Physical Damage to Spells while holding a Shield/wielding a Two Handed Weapon/Dual Wielding (previously up to 17 to 24).
    • Adds 24 to 35 Chaos Damage to Spells while holding a Shield/wielding a Two Handed Weapon/Dual Wielding (previously up to 17 to 24).
    • 8% chance to Gain Unholy Might for 4 seconds on Melee Kill (previously up to 5%).
    • Minions have 15% increased Movement Speed (previously up to 10%).
    • 25% reduced Effect of Curses on you while on Consecrated Ground (previously up to 15%).
    • 20% chance to Avoid Elemental Ailments while you have Elusive (previously up to 10%).
    • Enemies Hindered by you have 35% reduced Life Regeneration rate (previously up to 20%).
    • Enemies Blinded by you have 30% reduced Critical Strike Chance (previously up to 20%).

Legion Revamp

  • Added four Crystals of Permutation to the Domain of Timeless Conflict, which you can place Enshrouded Items into.
  • Enshrouded items can be transformed to another Unique of the same item class, with a Vestigial implicit modifier derived from the Enshrouded item.
  • Killing monsters in the Domain will charge the Enshrouded Item. When the Enshrouded item is fully charged you can interact with the Crystal of Permutation to drop the new item.
  • Added the following new Currency items that can drop from Legion Generals in Map areas which can Enshroud Unique items: Karui Enshrouding Crystal, Imperial Enshrouding Crystal, Maraketh Enshrouding Crystal, Templar Enshrouding Crystal, and Vaal Enshrouding Crystal. These items cannot drop in the Domain of Timeless Conflict.
  • Legion encounters will now automatically break out all Monsters in the encounter if you have broken out 90% of the total Monsters, as well as Chests, Sergeants, and Generals.
  • Unrelenting Timeless Emblems have been removed, existing items have been converted to regular Timeless Emblems.
  • The modifiers applied by Timeless Emblems have been adjusted, the new modifiers applied by each Emblem are as follows:
    • Timeless Eternal Emblem:
      • 10% increased Experience Gain.
      • 20% increased Reward progress from Monster Kills.
      • Monsters deal 10% increased Damage.
      • Monsters have 10% increased Toughness for each time they have been Revived.
      • Monsters have 10% increased Accuracy Rating for each time they have been Revived.
      • Monsters have 5% increased Area of Effect for each time they have been Revived.
    • Timeless Karui Emblem:
      • 10% increased Experience Gain.
      • 20% increased Reward progress from Monster Kills.
      • Monsters deal 10% increased Damage.
      • Monsters have 10% increased Toughness for each time they have been Revived.
      • Monsters gain 50% increased Critical Strike Chance each time they are Revived.
      • Monsters gain +5% to Critical Strike Multiplier each time they are Revived
    • Timeless Maraketh Emblem:
      • 10% increased Experience Gain.
      • 20% increased Reward progress from Monster Kills.
      • Monsters deal 10% increased Damage.
      • Monsters have 10% increased Toughness for each time they have been Revived.
      • Monsters have 5% increased Attack and Cast Speed for each time they have been Revived.
    • Timeless Templar Emblem:
      • 10% increased Experience Gain.
      • 20% increased Reward progress from Monster Kills.
      • Monsters deal 10% increased Damage.
      • Monsters have 10% increased Toughness for each time they have been Revived.
      • Monsters Penetrate 3% of Enemy Elemental Resistances for each time they have been Revived.
    • Timeless Vaal Emblem:
      • 10% increased Experience Gain.
